About Us


This is small Machine learning project created by the students of the Vellore Institute of technology - Amaravthi on the hackathon conducted by the Georgia University. This application takes the information from the client to predict whether he/she is eligible to receive loan from bank based on their financial history. This Application uses Machine learning algorithm Random forest with Adaboost technique to predict with a pre-trained model of around 18k rows of data.
